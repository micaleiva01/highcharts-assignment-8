Unemployment in the United States in 2022

By Micaela Leiva.

Unemployment is an issue for a lot of people over the age of twenty-five years old in the United States. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics over six thousand people over the age of twenty five were unemployed in 2021 in the U.S. White males reached the highest level of unemployment in 2021 with 2,461 individuals being unemployed. Unemployment is a problem for individuals as it not only damages their economic situation but it also affects their mental health as well.
